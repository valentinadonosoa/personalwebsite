Valentina Donoso is a student at Parsons School of Design from Santa Cruz, Bolivia. She is majoring in graphic design and minoring in creative coding. She is passionate about using her creativity to solve problems and create meaningful designs, and enjoys exploring the intersection of technology, design, and experimenting with new tools and techniques. Through her studies at Parsons, Valentina is developing her skills and building a strong foundation in both graphic design and creative coding, which will enable her to pursue a variety of exciting career opportunities in the future.
